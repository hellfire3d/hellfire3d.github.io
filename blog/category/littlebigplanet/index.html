 May the Fifth be with you
May 5, 2024

It’s May!

I’m pretty shocked by how fast time seems to be moving to me. I still feel like it’s the start of the year but the summer solstice is going to be right around the corner.

Last Wednesday I had a video gig at Wheaton College, filming their Jazz Band. The ensemble played a range of tracks from the mid 1900s. I took my fianc√©e and she described the show as “life-changing.” I had a lot of fun filming this, it was my first time in this theater. I was pretty intimidated but having completed it, I’m satisfied with the results. You can watch the video on YouTube here.

On Friday, we went running with our friend Rory. I’ve been trying to get more active. I intend on keeping up with this during the summer, I absolutely need it.

On Saturday, I reopened one of my old YouTube channels. It’s the account that I used to stream LittleBigPlanet content on. I stupidly didn’t pay attention to the fact you can only rename the channel twice within a 14-day span on YouTube, so currently it is “hellfire2345.tv” and not “hellfire.tv,” as I made two typos and saved it both times. Lame. (both the website and me)

That game, unfortunately, had its servers closed in April, which was a great loss to lots of people I know. I want to make some sort of a long-form post talking about LittleBigPlanet, because my life would not be what it is without that game. I have a lot to reflect on with that, and a lot of stories to tell.
As a result of this close, I’ve been kindof getting back into LittleBigPlanet. I have a difficult relationship with that game, as it brings back equally good and bad memories. As I get older, I try to let go of the bad and keep the good. I tried making a custom character costume (like a skin) but gave up pretty quickly. I don’t have it in me much anymore :(

There’s some kind of science out there in regards to how long it takes a person to forget something after having not thought about it for an extended period of time. I’ve been noticing that every time I get in and out of that game, I end up losing most of the knowledge I have the next time I get back into it. And the crazy part is, I was the first person to mod that game[citation needed], and I tend to forget most of what I know, resulting in me pestering the few people I know who still do play that game.

Anyway, that’s my reason for opening a blog. I hope some day I don’t forget these years!
